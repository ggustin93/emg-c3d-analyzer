The system calculates:

- **RMS** (Root Mean Square)
- **MAV** (Mean Absolute Value)
- **MPF** (Mean Power Frequency)
- **MDF** (Median Frequency)
- **Contraction Duration**
- **Fatigue Index**
- **Compliance Score**
